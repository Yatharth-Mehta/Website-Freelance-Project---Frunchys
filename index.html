Who We Are

Frunchys is the premium snacking brand of Brother's Food Pvt Ltd, founded with a simple mission, to make healthy snacking delicious and convenient.

We specialise in freeze-dried fruits and vegetables that retain up to 98% of their original nutrients, colour, and flavour.

Our Mission

In a world full of ultra-processed snacks, we believe there's a better way. Frunchys proves healthy food doesn't compromise on taste.

Our state-of-the-art facility uses cutting-edge sublimation technology to preserve every piece perfectly.
